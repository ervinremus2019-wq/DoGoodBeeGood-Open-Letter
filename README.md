# #DoGoodBeeGood - Open Letter

**One Pixel. Billions in Change.**

Dear leaders of the global technology and online commerce community,

I am reaching out to you as innovators and leaders in your industries, with the hope that together we can make a meaningful difference in the global online community and beyond.

We are building a grassroots movement with a simple but powerful goal: to encourage every major online retailer and digital platform to give customers the opportunity to donate to charity when making a purchase.

Companies such as Amazon, eBay, Microsoft, Apple, Google, IBM, Salesforce, GoDaddy, Tucows, eNom, Citrix, OpenAI, X, Slack, Anthropic, Meta, Nvidia, and many others have transformed the world through technology and commerce. With their reach and influence, even a small change could create a massive positive impact.

All we are asking for is a small space at the checkout page — just a few pixels on the screen — where customers can choose to donate a small amount to charity.

For example:
> "Would you like to donate $1 to charity?"

This tiny addition could unlock billions of dollars in voluntary charitable contributions every year. Every online purchase would become an opportunity for people to do good.

We are not asking for the world.
We are simply asking for a few pixels.

We believe that great things can come from this simple yet revolutionary shift in the way we shop online.

I invite you to join this effort by supporting the creation of a campaign platform that can serve as the starting point and springboard for this movement. With your expertise and resources, we can build something that strengthens the relationship between companies and customers while creating measurable benefits for communities around the world.

Together we can create a future where doing good is built into the everyday digital experience.

Thank you for considering this initiative.

Yours faithfully,
Ervin Remus Radosavlevici
Founder — #DoGoodBeeGood
